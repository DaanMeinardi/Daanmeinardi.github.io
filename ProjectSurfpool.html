Summary

The challenge was great to learn to work for a client. Also I found in this process the most important thing was spreading the survey within the community. Even with a perfect quick survey, if you don't reach the right people, the data won't be useful. So from this I take that it is even more important to work on networking and profiling. Also for working together in the future it is important to send reminders to get responses on emails, since I once was waiting for over a week and the client just forgot. So I couldnt continue working. All in all a very usefull challenge and good experience.
